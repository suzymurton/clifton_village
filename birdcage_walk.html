The Birdcage Walk


The Birdcage Walk can be found in the graveyard of the St Andrews church. It takes its name from the arches lining the walkway and is perfect for a peaceful moment off the beaten track. The graveyard has been in use since the 12th century but was destroyed in the WWII Blitz. A beautiful sunken graveyard with a tree-tunnel archway is what can now be found.
